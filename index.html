About Me

Engineering Philosophy & Background

Academic Journey

My academic path combines rigorous engineering fundamentals with entrepreneurial thinking. At Duke, I've embraced the challenge of not just understanding how things work, but questioning why they work that way and how they could work better. This approach has shaped my goal to become an engineer who bridges technical excellence with market insight.

I believe engineering is fundamentally about identifying fixable problems in the world and developing the technical skills to create meaningful solutions. Great engineering starts with understanding the right problem to solve, then having the determination and technical expertise to solve it completely.
