Carbon Rim Impact Testing Machine

Designed, built, and tested a comprehensive UCI-protocol compliant testing machine for Boyd Cycling to evaluate carbon rim impact resistance. Machine capable of measuring forces upwards of 200J (minimum UCI requirement: 40J). Features winch activation system, electromagnetic door stopper release mechanism, and precision-guided steel impact plate with dual cable guidance system to eliminate friction and ensure accurate drop path.

200J+ Force Capacity 5x minimum UCI testing requirements
Complete Design Ownership Solo project from concept to testing
