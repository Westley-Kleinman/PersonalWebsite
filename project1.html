Carbon Rim Impact Testing Machine

UCI-Compliant Testing Equipment for Professional Cycling

Designed and manufactured a precision impact testing machine for Boyd Cycling during my Summer 2024 internship. This UCI-compliant equipment enables rigorous testing of carbon fiber bicycle rims with controlled 200J+ impact forces.

Project Overview

Engineering Challenge

The Problem

Boyd Cycling needed a reliable, UCI-compliant testing machine to validate the impact resistance of their carbon fiber bicycle rims. Existing solutions were either too expensive, lacked precision, or didn't meet the specific requirements of professional cycling regulations.

Design Requirements

  • UCI Compliance: Meet all International Cycling Union testing standards
  • 200J+ Impact Force: Generate controlled, repeatable high-energy impacts
  • Precision Control: Electromagnetic release system for consistent results
  • Safety Features: Comprehensive safety systems for operator protection
  • Data Collection: Integrated measurement and logging capabilities
  • Manufacturing Ready: Design for efficient production and maintenance

Technical Specifications

Impact Energy 200+ Joules
Drop Height Variable (0.5-2.0m)
Release System Electromagnetic

Research & Requirements

Conducted extensive research on UCI testing standards, analyzed existing testing methods, and collaborated with Boyd Cycling engineers to define precise requirements. Studied impact mechanics and material testing protocols.

Concept Development

Developed multiple design concepts focusing on precision, safety, and repeatability. Evaluated electromagnetic vs. mechanical release systems, analyzed force distribution mechanisms, and optimized the overall system architecture.

Technical Details

Engineering Implementation

Electromagnetic Release System

Precision electromagnetic release mechanism ensures consistent drop timing and eliminates human error. Digital control system allows for exact force calibration and repeatable test conditions.

Safety Systems

Comprehensive safety features including emergency stops, protective barriers, operator lockout systems, and fail-safe mechanisms to ensure complete operator protection during high-energy testing.

Data Collection

Integrated measurement systems capture impact force, deformation data, and failure modes. Digital logging enables comprehensive analysis and UCI compliance documentation.

Precision Manufacturing

Designed for manufacturing efficiency with standard components and clear assembly procedures. Modular design allows for easy maintenance and component replacement in production environments.
