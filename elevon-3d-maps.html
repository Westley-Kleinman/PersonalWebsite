Tri-Dimensional Topographic Maps

Entrepreneurial Venture with Provisional Patent

Designed and produced 3D-printed trail maps providing undistorted topographic visualization. Secured provisional patent and developed marketing strategy resulting in four-figure revenue with established partnerships including Visit Hot Springs and Arkansas NICA.

Project Overview

Four-year entrepreneurial venture combining technical innovation with strategic business development to create market-leading 3D topographic visualization solutions.

Innovation & Design

Converting 2D topographic limitations into 3D visualization solutions

The Challenge

Race directors and trail enthusiasts struggled with 2D map limitations for understanding terrain complexity. Traditional topographic maps couldn't effectively communicate elevation changes, trail difficulty, or spatial relationships needed for race planning and navigation.

Technical Solution

Designed and produced 3D-printed trail maps providing undistorted topographic visualization. Developed proprietary process using Fusion360 and applied digital manufacturing techniques, converting GIS elevation data into accurate, scaled terrain models that preserve true topographic relationships.

Innovation Impact

Created market-first solution combining real topographic elevation data with trail route information, difficulty classifications, and geographic accuracy while remaining cost-effective for small race events and custom applications.

Technical Specifications

Design Software Fusion360

Business Development

Market Strategy

Developed comprehensive marketing strategy targeting race directors, tourism organizations, and outdoor enthusiasts. Identified key value propositions including improved race planning capabilities and unique trophy/commemorative applications.

Strategic Partnerships

Established partnerships with Visit Hot Springs for tourism promotion and Arkansas NICA for youth cycling programs. These relationships expanded market reach and validated commercial viability while providing recurring revenue streams.

Revenue Achievement

Achieved consistent four-figure revenue through direct sales, custom commissions, and partnership agreements. Demonstrated sustainable business model with recurring customer relationships and referral-based growth over four years of operation.
