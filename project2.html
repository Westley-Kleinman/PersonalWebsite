Elevon - 3D Topographic Maps

From High School Project to Commercial Business

Founded and operate Elevon, transforming real elevation data into precision 3D printed topographic maps. Started as a high school project, now serving race directors with custom trophy maps and trail enthusiasts with detailed terrain models.

3+ Years Operating
Founder Business Owner
Active Current Status
Market Opportunity

Identifying the Need

Trail Visualization Challenge

Trail runners, cyclists, and race directors needed better ways to visualize and understand terrain elevation changes. Traditional 2D maps couldn't effectively communicate the physical challenge and beauty of trail routes, especially for race planning and participant preparation.

Race Director Pain Points

Event organizers struggled to create meaningful awards and course materials that truly represented their race routes. Generic trophies didn't capture the unique character of each course, and participants couldn't fully appreciate the terrain they'd conquered.

Technology Gap

While elevation data was readily available, there was no accessible way for individuals and small organizations to transform this data into physical, tangible representations. The technology existed but wasn't packaged for consumer use.

Business Solution

Elevon Implementation

Product Development

Developed a streamlined process to convert real topographic elevation data into 3D printable models. Created custom software workflows to process GPS coordinates, elevation data, and route information into accurate, scaled terrain representations suitable for 3D printing.

Manufacturing Process

Established reliable 3D printing operations with quality control measures to ensure consistent, high-quality output. Developed finishing techniques to enhance durability and visual appeal. Optimized print settings for different map sizes and detail levels.

Customer Experience

Built customer ordering system and established relationships with race directors for custom trophy production. Created clear communication processes for custom orders, timeline management, and quality assurance. Developed packaging and shipping procedures.
